Nexc is a free and open-source workout tracker designed with privacy in mind.<br>
Create fully personalized workouts assembled from a rich dataset of hundreds of exercises — each exercise paired with images and step-by-step instructions covering setup and execution.<br>
Schedule single sessions, filter exercises by equipment, muscle group or difficulty with one tap. During workouts, track every set, rep, rest interval, and load in real time.<br>

<b>📊 Activity Tracking</b><br>
Log your workouts with its exercises, sets, reps, and duration.<br><br>
<b>🎯 Progress Monitoring</b><br>
Visualize your progress over time with insightful charts and statistics.<br><br>
<b>📅 Workout Planning</b><br>
Create and customize workout plans tailored to your fitness goals.<br><br>
<b>✨️ Rich dataset of exercises with images</b><br>
Access a comprehensive library of 800+ exercises with detailed instructions and high-quality demonstration images for proper form and technique.<br><br>
<b>📱 Offline-First</b><br>
Track workouts and access all features without an internet connection.<br><br>
<b>🔒 Privacy-Focused</b><br>
Your data is stored locally on your device, ensuring that your personal information remains private and secure.<br><br>
<b>🎨 Material Design 3 Expressive</b><br>
Enjoy a sleek and modern user interface that enhances your experience.<br><br>
